Konni EcoTourism



Ganesh Kumar,He is a member of the Kerala Legislative Assembly (MLA) from the Konni constituency, has been actively supporting ecotourism in the region. As a local politician, he recognizes the importance of promoting sustainable development and preserving the natural beauty of the area. In 2017, Kumar launched the 'Konni Eco Tourism Project' in collaboration with the Kerala Tourism Department. The project aims to promote responsible tourism by creating awareness among tourists about the importance of conserving the environment and supporting local communities.

As part of the project, Kumar has been advocating for the development of infrastructure and facilities to support ecotourism activities, such as trekking trails, bird-watching spots, and wildlife safaris. He has also been working to promote sustainable practices among local businesses, such as the use of eco-friendly products and waste management. Kumar's efforts have been well-received by both locals and tourists, and have helped to increase awareness about the importance of responsible tourism. By supporting ecotourism, he is helping to create a sustainable future for the region while also promoting its natural beauty and cultural heritage.
